Our Leadership

Dr. Ishwar V. Basavaraddi

Patron-in-Chief

A global authority in Yoga science and policy, Dr. Basavaraddi served as the Director of MDNIY for 18 years and as the Adviser to the Government of India. As the chief architect of the Common Yoga Protocol and former head of the WHO Collaborating Centre, he has spent over three decades standardizing Yoga education and integrating traditional wisdom into modern global healthcare systems.

Satyam Tiwari

Founder & President

A scientist and doctoral researcher at IIT Mandi, Satyam is a global pioneer in Yoga-AI integration and the inventor of the world’s first Svara-based biofeedback hardware. With over 15 years of rigorous Yoga Sadhana, he has bridged the gap between ancient respiratory science and modern deep-tech to empower a digital wellness ecosystem spanning 150+ countries, transforming how the world validates Yoga.
